ABOUT

WRENCH FUND was started by five uwaterloo engineering grads with the goal of inspiring young engineers and increasing the amount of hardware built in canada


Read our master plan here

THE WRENCH FUND GRANT

Each year, we offer a $300 grant to a uwaterloo capstone team to help them overcome financial barriers and inspire them to pursue their project further

WHAT WE ARE LOOKING FOR

We want to support teams that are:

  • Working on hardware
  • Passionate about solving problems
  • Curious about commercialization and taking their project to the next level

HOW TO APPLY

Check back in fall 2026 for the 2027 grant application details

PAST RECIPIENTS

  • 2025 | Salico: A novel automated harvesting solution for sea asparagus, a healthy, delicious, and environmentally beneficial plant.
  • 2026 | Celebrim: A custom ring manufacturing platform focused on making jewellery ring designing efficient and accessible to makers of all skill levels, removing the tedious process of current prototyping methods.
